About

My name is Katrijn Oelbrandt. I am a graphic design student in my second bachelor at LUCA School of Arts in Ghent, Belgium. In this portfolio you'll see what work I'm recently making and what I'm interested in. 

PROJECTS


Project 1 — A manual about 29 failures of making a fried egg and a final succeeded one associated with the myth of Sisyphus.


Project 2 — A new design for Lieven Martens Moana, Dolphins Into The Future / Songs Of Gold, Incandescent.



Project 3 — A French newsmagzine about the fact that Paris can easely be visited by surfing on the internet.



Project 4 —  A new and modern version of the Belgian Constitution with a chosen article as an attachment.


Project 5 —  A newspaper that represents only the positive aspects of Donald Trump on the basis of dingbats from McDonald’s advertisements. Those dingbats replace the negative words into positive ones. The focus on Trump his smile is associated with the slogans of toothpaste. The hierarchy between articles that may or may not be read is strong.
